Oliver Baumann

Bio

Oliver Baumann is an Associate Professor of Management at the Strategic Organization Design Unit, University of Southern Denmark. His research focuses on the intersection of organization theory, strategy, and innovation management. In particular, he is interested in the strategic and organizational implications of complexity as well as in the effects of organizational design on innovation and performance.

Oliver received a Ph.D. and MBR in Business Economics from LMU Munich, a Diplom-Wirtschaftsingenieur (M.S. in Business Engineering) from Karlsruhe Institute of Technology, and a M.S. in Engineering Management from Portland State University. He was a visiting scholar at the Wharton School and an Assistant Professor of Management at LMU Munich before joining the University of Southern Denmark.
